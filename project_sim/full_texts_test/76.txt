The National Policy for Adaptation to Climate Change developed in 2005 established objectives for adaptation and key actions that are needed to address climate change in various sectors; although progress in the adoption of the policy has been slow. The University of the Bahamas has established a Climate Change Initiative (CCI) to conduct research relevant to the issue.
