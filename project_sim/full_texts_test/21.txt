In New Providence, transport challenges tend to be similar to those faced by many urban areas around the world. Public ground transportation is limited to privately-owned buses referred to as ÒjitneysÓ operating on government defined routes and government regulated fares. There is limited bus coverage for unprofitable routes and during the evening hours. This has led to over-reliance on personal vehicles and traffic congestion. Additionally, cycling is almost non-existent particularly with a lack of designated cycling lane	21
